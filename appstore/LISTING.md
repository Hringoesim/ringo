## Description  (≤4000 chars)

One plan. Every country. Ringo is the global eSIM that keeps you connected the
moment you land — no roaming bills, no hunting for a local SIM, no swapping cards.
Keep your own number, add a local one, and travel the world on a single plan.

Install Ringo in minutes and you're ready for 180+ countries. Your data just
works when you arrive, and one simple monthly bill covers all of it.

WHY RINGO
• One plan, 180+ countries — no roaming, no daily passes, no surprise fees
• Keep your number — bring your existing number with you, or add a local one
• Instant eSIM — set up on your iPhone in minutes, no physical SIM
• Transparent pricing — flat monthly plans, cancel anytime
• Private and secure — sign in with Apple, unlock with Face ID

BUILT FOR HOW YOU TRAVEL
• Frequent flyers, remote workers, and anyone living across borders
• Land, connect, and get on with your trip — data is ready when you are
• Manage everything from one clean dashboard: data, numbers, and your plan

CHOOSE YOUR PLAN
• Essentials — 10 GB, calls & SMS in 60+ countries, 1 number
• Plus — 50 GB, unlimited in-network calls, 180+ countries
• Pro — unlimited data, a virtual + ported numbers, priority network
• Unlimited — everything in Pro, more numbers, and partner perks

KEEP OR ADD A NUMBER
Bring your existing number to Ringo, or add a fresh local number for the places
you go most. Calls, texts, and data — all in one app.

MEMBERSHIP THAT REWARDS EXPLORING
The more of the world you connect to, the more your Ringo membership grows —
with perks that get better as you travel.

SUBSCRIPTION DETAILS
Ringo plans are auto-renewing monthly subscriptions. Payment is charged to your
Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. Your subscription automatically renews
unless it is cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
Manage or cancel any time in your Apple ID account settings. Prices are shown in
the app before you buy and may vary by region.

Privacy Policy: https://ringoesim.com/privacy
